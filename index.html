A bit about Me.

I am passionate about data and the stories it tells. For four years I worked as a factory hand working my way up to Health and Safety Representative for the workers there, this involved supporting the managers by liasing between them and the workers, running weekly meetings and researching and organising safety practices in the workplace based on national guidlines. I desired change and decided that starting my own business in software/app development was the way forward. I did that for over a year and it involved speaking to and managing my time with clients, pitch meetings, design and data management. With the birth of my daughter I am ready to jump into something a bit more stable and put my skills to use in data technology.
